How It Works

Simple, personal, and done quickly. Here's what happens after you place an order.

01
🎨
Pick a Template

Browse our themed templates on the home page. Click Visit to preview the live website, then hit Order when you find the right one.

02
📝
Fill the Order Form

Tell us the birthday person's name, their date, and a message from you. The form takes under 2 minutes.

03
💬
We Contact You on WhatsApp

After placing your order, we'll message you on WhatsApp to collect photos and any extra details. We work on your site and share a preview before you pay.

04
🔗
Get Your Link

Once you approve the site, we send you the shareable link. Share it with the birthday person — that's it!

Common Questions

How long does it take to get the website?

It depends on when you send us the photos and how busy we are. Most orders are completed within a day or two. We'll give you a clear timeline on WhatsApp.

What photos do I need to send?

Any photos you want included — could be 2–3 pictures of you both, or solo photos of the birthday person. We'll guide you on WhatsApp.

When do I pay?

After we send you a preview and you're happy with it. We share the payment link on WhatsApp. Only ₹99!

Can I request changes after seeing the preview?

Yes — one round of changes is included. If you need more edits, we'll discuss on WhatsApp.

How long does the link stay active?

We keep the website live for at least 30 days. After that, reach out if you'd like it extended.
