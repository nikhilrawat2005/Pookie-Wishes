Common Questions

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 24–72 hours after you submit your photos and messages via the personalisation form.

What exactly will I receive?

You will receive a unique, private URL (link) to an interactive greeting website featuring your chosen template, personalised with your content.

Can I see a preview before payment?

We provide live demos for all templates so you can experience exactly how the final page will behave before you buy.

What is your refund policy?

Since these are personalised digital services, we offer a 50% refund if the cancellation is requested before the build process starts. No refunds are possible after the final link is delivered.

Is my content safe?

Yes. All photos and messages are stored securely and used only for your personalised page. We do not process any prohibited or adult content.
