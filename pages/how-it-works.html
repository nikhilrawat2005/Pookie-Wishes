Common Questions

How long does it take?

It depends on how quickly you send us photos and how many orders we have at the time. We'll give you an honest timeline on WhatsApp after you order. Most orders are completed within a day or two of receiving all photos.

What photos should I send?

2–4 photos work best — could be photos of you both, or solo photos of the birthday person. Don't worry if you're not sure which to send, we'll guide you.

When do I pay?

After we send you the preview link and you're happy with it. We share the UPI/payment link on WhatsApp at that point. Prices vary by design — starting at ₹150.

Can I request changes to the preview?

Yes. One round of changes is included at no extra cost. If you need more after that, we'll discuss on WhatsApp.

How long does the link stay active?

We keep websites live for at least 30 days. If you need it extended, just message us on WhatsApp or email.

Can I order multiple templates?

Yes! Add multiple templates to your cart and checkout together. Each one will be treated as a separate order and personalised individually.
