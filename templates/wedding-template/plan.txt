Project: Premium Wedding Wishes Template ("Royal Nuptials")
Template Folder: templates/wedding-template/

1. Theme Overview:
   - A sophisticated, elegant, and interactive wedding congratulatory experience.
   - Purpose: For friends/family to send to a couple on their wedding day or anniversary.
   - Aesthetic: Luxury invitation style, ivory/gold colors, floral animations.

2. Visual Concepts:
   - Background: Soft cream/ivory with a subtle animated lace pattern.
   - Floating Elements: White flower petals (Cherry blossoms or Roses) and subtle light rays.
   - Fonts: 'Playfair Display' (Serif) for headings, 'Alex Brush' (Cursive) for decorative text.

3. Key Journey/Sections:
   - The Royal Mail: An elegant envelope animation that opens on click to reveal the invitation content.
   - The Journey: A horizontal scroll or parallax section of 3-5 photos with Polaroid-style frames.
   - The Message: A heartfelt handwritten-style letter appearing with a typewriter effect.
   - The Celebration: Dynamic confetti (hearts, rings, petals) when the user reaches the end.
   - Interactive Gift: A premium gift box that "unwraps" to reveal a personalized voucher or quote.

4. Suggested External Assets (STICKERS COLLECTION):
   - Wedding Icon Set (PNG/SVG): Intertwined Rings, Bouquet, Champagne Glasses, Wedding Cake, Heart Lock.
   - Floral Accents: 4 corner floral arrangements (White/Pastel Roses) in high-res PNG with transparency.
   - Animated Stickers (Lottie/GIF): Sparkling diamond rings, pair of flying white doves, a pulsing golden heart.
   - Calligraphy Text Stickers: "Always & Forever", "Mr. & Mrs.", "Happily Ever After", "Just Married".
   - Background Music: A soft piano or acoustic violin track (e.g., Cannon in D or similar vibes).

5. Next Steps:
   - Developer to set up base index.html, style.css, and script.js.
   - User to provide the specific sticker pack/PNGs for the floral theme.
   - Implementation of "WeddingDoodleEngine" for randomized but elegant sticker placement.
